Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calibrate the reticle?

Tap the Calibrate button, point at a target of known size, enter the target size and distance, then adjust the bracket spread until it matches your target through the scope.

What units are supported?

Dial In supports both MOA (Minute of Angle) and MRAD (Milliradian) reticle overlays.

Does the app require internet access?

No. Dial In works completely offline.

What devices are supported?

Dial In is designed for iPhone and requires iOS 13.0 or later.

Privacy Policy

Dial In does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app uses your device camera and microphone solely to display the live reticle overlay and enable video recording. No data leaves your device.
